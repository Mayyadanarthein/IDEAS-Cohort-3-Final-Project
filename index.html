About The Project

Born from our journey with AI LA IDEAS Cohort 3, this project aims to simplify how people evaluate news credibility. Starting with a Kaggle dataset and our growing knowledge of machine learning, we built this analyzer that makes credible information more accessible. While our project began as a final assignment, it's grown into something we're truly passionate about - a tool that can help anyone navigate today's complex media landscape.
